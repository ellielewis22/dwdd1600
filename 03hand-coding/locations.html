Our Favorite Locations

Thrifting is all about the thrill of the hunt, and finding the best stores can make all the difference. Whether you're looking for budget-friendly fashion, vintage treasures, or secondhand home décor, we’ve rounded up our favorite thrift store locations to help you score the best finds. Below, you’ll find our top picks for thrift stores in Utah, as well as some must-visit spots across the country.

Best Thrift Stores in Utah

Preloved
With new booths weekly, Preloved is your stop if you like finding gently used clothing in a clean, boutique feel.
Deseret Industries
One of Utah’s most well-known thrift chains, Deseret Industries (DI) offers everything from clothing to furniture at unbeatable prices. Plus, your purchases support job training and community programs.
The Other Side Thrift Boutique
A nonprofit thrift store with a boutique feel, featuring stylish secondhand clothing and home goods. Proceeds help support addiction recovery programs.
